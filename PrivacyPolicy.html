Ticket Wizard

Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ticket Wizard collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information when users access the platform, create accounts, purchase tickets, or interact with event services.

1. Information We Collect

Ticket Wizard may collect limited personal information such as email address and account details required to support the functionality of the platform.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information collected is used to create and manage user accounts, process ticket bookings, send confirmation emails, generate QR code tickets, validate entry at events, and prevent fraud such as duplicate or reused tickets. Information may also be used to improve platform functionality and user experience.

3. Payment Information

Ticket Wizard uses Stripe to process payments securely. Payment card details are not stored directly by Ticket Wizard. Users are redirected to Stripe’s secure payment environment when completing a purchase.

4. Ticket Validation and Fraud Prevention

Each ticket generated by Ticket Wizard includes a unique QR code and usage status. Tickets can only be used once. Promoters may validate tickets at the point of entry to confirm authenticity and prevent ticket duplication or fraudulent reuse.

5. Sharing of Information

Ticket Wizard does not sell personal information to third parties. Information may be shared only where necessary with trusted service providers, such as payment processors, or with event promoters where required for event management, attendance monitoring, and customer support.

6. Data Security

Reasonable measures are taken to protect personal information from unauthorised access, misuse, or loss. These measures may include session-based authentication, controlled system access, and secure handling of ticket and order data.

7. Cookies and Session Data

Ticket Wizard may use cookies or session technologies to keep users logged in, maintain account sessions, and support core platform functionality. These technologies help the site operate correctly and improve the user experience.

8. User Rights

Users may request to review or update their account information. Users may also choose whether to create an account or continue through guest checkout where available.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions about Ticket Wizard, users can contact the platform through the available support options provided within the website.
